About Me


Hello World!
Welcome to my Website! My name is Michael Abramo, and I am here because I eat, sleep, and breathe Decentralized Finance. I am an aspiring Blockchain Developer who's here to fight for a more honest and transparent economic future that works for everyone, not just the Top 1%. Onwards!
